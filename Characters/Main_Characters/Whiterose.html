Whiterose is the leader of the Dark Army, a Chinese hacker group. She is a transgender woman masquerading as the male Minister Zhi Zhang, the Chinese Minister of State Security. She is played by BD Wong.

Trivia

  • Her hacking skills are not yet known. She has stated that she "hacks time". (“eps1.7_wh1ter0se.m4v”)
  • The way Whiterose's legal name is given in the show is incorrect. As she is Chinese where they put surnames before personal names when giving full names the name should be referred to as Zhang Zhi, not the other way around that's used in the show.
